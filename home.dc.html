Environmental Epidemiologist

Joyce Lin, PhD

I study how environmental exposures across the life course shape trajectories of healthy aging and chronic disease.

My work integrates environmental epidemiology, exposure science, biomonitoring, and causal inference to understand how exposures during sensitive developmental periods influence health decades later. A central goal is to identify biological and social pathways that remain open to intervention across the lifespan.
