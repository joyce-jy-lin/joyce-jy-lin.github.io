# joyce-jy-lin.github.io

Personal academic website for Joyce J.Y. Lin, PhD — environmental epidemiologist, postdoctoral research fellow at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.

Live site: https://joyce-jy-lin.github.io/

## Structure

- `index.html` — SEO landing page; redirects visitors to `home.dc.html`
- `home.dc.html` — main landing page (bio, headshot, research areas)
- `publications.dc.html` — publications by research area
- `talks.dc.html` — talks and presentations
- `media.dc.html` — media coverage
- `cv.dc.html` — CV (print-to-PDF)
- `teaching.dc.html` — teaching
- `contact.dc.html` — contact and links
- `support.js` — runtime required by all pages (keep at root)
- `images/` — headshot
- `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt` — search engine indexing
