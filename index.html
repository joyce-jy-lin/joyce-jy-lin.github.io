Joyce J.Y. Lin, PhD

Also known as Joyce Lin, Joyce Lin PhD, and Joyce J.Y. Lin.

Environmental Epidemiologist · Postdoctoral Research Fellow, Department of Environmental Health, Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. PhD, Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health.

Joyce Lin studies how environmental exposures across the life course shape trajectories of healthy aging and chronic disease, integrating environmental epidemiology, exposure science, biomonitoring, and causal inference.
